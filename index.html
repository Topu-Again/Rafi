You Have Chosen Finn!!

You have chosen to be lucky!!! Finn’s personality is simple but powerful: he wants to do what’s right, protect people, and prove himself as a true hero. He often rushes into danger without thinking too much, which sometimes gets him into trouble—but also makes him fearless.

You Have Chosen Jake!!

You have chosen to be wise!!! Personality-wise, Jake is much more relaxed than Finn. He’s chill, humorous, and often prefers taking life easy instead of stressing about hero duties. He likes music, food, lounging around, and giving slightly goofy but surprisingly wise advice.
